Monthly Mortage Calculator For Fixed-rate Loan

Imagine that you are planning to buy your first home and want to have a rough idea of your monthly cost.

Let's look at the estimated monthly payments on a fixed-rate mortgage using the Shiny App: Monthly Mortgage Calculator for Fixed-rate Loan

Monthly Mortgage Calculator for Fixed-rate Loan

Sample Monthly Mortgage Calculation

principal = 250000
years = 30
dur = 30 * 12
int = 2.99 / 100 / 12
monthlyMortgage = principal * int*(1+int)^dur / ((1+int)^dur - 1)
